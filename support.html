常见问题

导出视频时没有声音？

TimeLapse 的延时视频为图片序列合成，原始帧不含音频，导出视频默认无声音，这是正常现象。

相机权限被拒绝怎么办？

请前往 iPhone「设置」→「TimeLapse」→ 开启「相机」权限，然后重新启动应用。

如何删除某一帧？

在项目编辑页面，长按缩略图可进入多选模式，勾选后点击删除按钮即可。

数据会上传到云端吗？

不会。所有照片和项目数据均仅保存在您的设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器。

FAQ

Why is there no audio in the exported video?

TimeLapse compiles a sequence of photos into a video. Since individual frames don't contain audio, the exported video is silent by default — this is expected behavior.

Camera permission was denied — what should I do?

Go to iPhone Settings → TimeLapse and enable the Camera permission, then relaunch the app.

How do I delete a frame?

On the project editing screen, long-press a thumbnail to enter multi-select mode, then select the frames you want to remove and tap the delete button.

Is my data uploaded to the cloud?

No. All photos and project data are stored only on your device and are never sent to any server.
